    Artist Creates
    75 Hours of Illustration in
    Under Two
    Minutes

    Check out this incredible time-lapse video of Parisian artist, Guillaume Cornet, creating 75 hours of illustration in under two minutes. Using a fine-tip pen on a 70cm x 50cm blank canvas, Cornet brings characters and detailed scenery to life. Later, he colors the scene, the whole process taking over 75 hours. The final product: a piece entitled Parisian Neighbourhood. The subjects and characters within the piece are inspired by real life experiences that the artist had growing up in a similar Parisian neighborhood. You can get a print of this in his Society6 store.

    Cornet’s style is impressive and filled with personality. His skill and dedication are admirable as well. Artists such as Cornet inspire us all to create similar projects and share them with the world. Thanks to Guillaume Cornet for this piece and the video documenting it. It is a truly incredible thing to witness.
